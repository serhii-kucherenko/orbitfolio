# Orbitfolio design lab rubric

Each `/test/n` variant is scored 0–10 on five axes. **Composite** = weighted average.

| Axis | Weight | What “10” looks like |
|------|--------|----------------------|
| Coolness | 30% | Immediate wow — space FX, motion craft, memorable first viewport |
| Comprehensiveness | 20% | CV content complete: summary, skills, experience, contact, projects |
| Uniqueness | 20% | Layout/UX/components clearly distinct from other variants |
| Motion & FX | 15% | Intentional animation; reduced-motion parallel; no jank |
| Clarity & Hireability | 15% | Recruiter can answer “who / what / proof” in <10 seconds |

## Promotion

Champion (`/`) = highest composite. Ties break on Coolness, then Hireability.

## Rules

- Variants must differ in **structure**, not only colors
- Prefer modern 2026 craft: scroll storytelling, WebGL/canvas space, GSAP/Framer, Lenis where useful
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- No purple-on-white cliché; no cream+terracotta; brand Serhii first in hero
