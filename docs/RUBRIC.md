# Orbitfolio design lab rubric

Each of the 100 `/test/n` variants is scored 0–10 on five axes. **Composite** = weighted average.

| Axis | Weight | What “10” looks like |
|------|--------|----------------------|
| Coolness | 30% | Immediate wow — award-grade first viewport (motion, spatial, or typographic craft) |
| Comprehensiveness | 20% | CV complete: summary, skills, experience, contact, projects |
| Uniqueness | 20% | Layout/UX/components clearly distinct from other variants |
| Motion & FX | 15% | Intentional animation; reduced-motion parallel; no jank or UX bugs |
| Clarity & Hireability | 15% | Recruiter can answer “who / what / proof” in &lt;10 seconds |

## Promotion

Champion (`/`) = highest composite. Ties break on Coolness, then Hireability.

## Rules

- Variants must differ in **structure**, not only colors
- Prefer modern 2026 craft: scroll storytelling, WebGL/R3F, GSAP/Framer, Lenis, shader atmosphere
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- No purple-on-white cliché; no cream+terracotta; brand **Serhii** first in hero
- No UI/UX bugs: focus order, contrast, tap targets, no trapped scroll, links work
- Learn: Eta Hybrid cells must cite prior lessons and improve composite over the previous hybrid
