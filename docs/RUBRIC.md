# Orbitfolio design lab rubric

Each of the 100 `/test/n` designs is scored 0–10 on five axes. **Overall score** = weighted average.

| Axis | Weight | What “10” looks like |
|------|--------|----------------------|
| Coolness | 30% | Immediate wow — award-grade first screen (motion, space, or typography) |
| Completeness | 20% | Full resume content: summary, skills, experience, contact, projects |
| Uniqueness | 20% | Layout and interaction clearly different from other designs |
| Motion & effects | 15% | Intentional animation; calm version when the user prefers less motion; no jank or usability bugs |
| Clarity & hireability | 15% | A recruiter can answer “who / what / proof” in under 10 seconds |

## Promotion

Homepage champion = highest overall score. Ties break on Coolness, then hireability.

## Rules

- Designs must differ in **structure**, not only colors
- Prefer modern 2026 craft: scroll storytelling, browser 3D (Three.js / React Three Fiber), Framer Motion or GreenSock animation, smooth scroll where useful, shader atmosphere
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- No purple-on-white cliché; no cream+terracotta; brand **Serhii** first in the hero
- No interface bugs: focus order, contrast, tap targets, no trapped scroll, links work
- Learn: Hybrid team designs (#86–#100) must cite earlier lessons and beat the previous Hybrid overall score
